# SolPay Server — Industry and Trend Blog

Industry posts build authority and attract top-of-funnel readers who are not yet looking
for SolPay specifically but are thinking about the problems SolPay solves.

The rule: SolPay is mentioned as relevant context, not as the subject. If SolPay is the
hero of every sentence, this is a product post — use `blog-product` instead. Industry
posts earn credibility by being genuinely informative first.

**Before starting:** Load:
- `product-marketing/SKILL.md` — voice, pillars, audience, banned phrases
- `seo-marketing/SKILL.md` — keyword rules, on-page requirements

---

## What Industry Posts Do

- **Educate** readers on a development that affects how they think about payments or crypto
- **Position** SolPay within a broader trend without pitching it directly
- **Attract** links from other publications who want to cite a thoughtful take
- **Build** search presence for informational queries that lead to product-intent queries later

---

## Topic Categories

### Regulatory and Policy
Stablecoin legislation, CBDC developments, KYC/AML changes, crypto payment regulations.
Frame: what this means for merchants and developers, how self-hosted infrastructure is
affected, what the smart response is.

### Solana Ecosystem
Network upgrades, new tooling, validator changes, major protocol launches, DeFi milestones.
Frame: what this means for payment use cases on Solana, where the opportunities are.

### Stablecoin Landscape
USDC and USDT adoption, new stablecoin entrants, issuer risks, stablecoin payment volume data.
Frame: what the data says, how this changes the settlement calculus for merchants.

### Crypto Payments Broadly
Mainstream payment adoption data, card network vs. crypto settlement comparisons,
merchant acceptance trends, high-risk merchant deplatforming stories.
Frame: the macro shift, where it leads, what infrastructure it demands.

### Self-Hosting and Open Source
Movement trends in self-hosted software, data sovereignty conversations, BTCPay Server
ecosystem developments, broader open-source payment tool adoption.
Frame: why self-hosting is having a moment, what it means for the payments stack.

## Step 1: Topic and Angle Selection

1. If the topic is given, proceed. If vague, ask for the specific event or trend to cover.

2. Web search for recent coverage of the topic. Pull the most relevant 3-5 sources.

3. Identify the SolPay angle — the lens through which SolPay can add a perspective
   that isn't already in the existing coverage:
   - A data point others haven't highlighted
   - A consequence for a specific audience (merchants, developers) that others glossed over
   - An honest counterpoint to the dominant narrative
   - A "what does this mean for self-hosted infrastructure specifically" framing

4. Identify a target keyword. Industry posts often target informational keywords:
   - "stablecoin payments 2025"
   - "Solana merchant adoption"
   - "self-hosted payment gateway benefits"

5. Present angle and keyword for approval before outlining.

---

## Step 2: Outline

- **2-3 title options** — specific, informational framing, keyword near front
- **Meta description** — 140-160 chars, informational, what the reader learns
- **Opening hook** — the specific data point, event, or tension this post opens with
- **H2 structure** — each section annotated with what it covers and which source it draws from
- **SolPay mention plan** — where in the post SolPay appears and how (context, not pitch)
- **Data and source plan** — which specific stats or facts will be cited
- **CTA** — soft and relevant (join the newsletter, read related post, learn about self-hosted payments)

**Wait for outline approval before drafting.**

---

## Step 3: Draft

### The SolPay mention rule

SolPay should appear naturally in 1-3 places:
- Once as context in the body ("projects like SolPay Server are built for exactly this
  kind of environment")
- Once in the CTA at the close ("if you're looking at self-hosted Solana infrastructure,
  SolPay Server is worth a look")
- Optionally once in the intro if the post's angle is specifically about a problem SolPay
  addresses

Never more than three mentions. The post is about the industry, not the product.

### Source and data rules
- Cite actual statistics with sources in the body
- Link to primary sources (Solana Foundation, Chainalysis, CFTC reports, company
  announcements) over secondary aggregators
- If you cannot find a primary source for a statistic, do not use the statistic
- Note the publication date of data — stale data undermines credibility

### Voice for industry posts
- Can be slightly more analytical and editorial than product posts
- Take a position on the trend — "this matters because..." rather than just reporting
- Acknowledge competing interpretations where they exist
- No em dashes, no banned phrases, no hype

### Length
- Regulatory/policy analysis: 1,200-2,000 words
- Ecosystem roundup or trend overview: 800-1,400 words
- Deep analytical piece: 1,500-2,500 words
